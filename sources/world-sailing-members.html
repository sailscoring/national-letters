World Sailing Membership

      Information on the process of becoming a World Sailing Member, Association or Class along with subscription categories and rates.

      Become a Member National Authority

      The World Sailing Draft Constitution for Potential New Members includes a draft constitution and sample letter templates for use by prospective Member National Authorities (MNA). Please contact the World Sailing Executive Office to discuss how to become a World Sailing MNA or if you have questions about the application process.

      Become a World Sailing Class

      There are many advantages of becoming a World Sailing Class that include holding a World Championships, providing Race Officials and the services offered by the World Sailing Committees and Technical and Offshore Department.
